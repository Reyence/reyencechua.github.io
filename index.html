About Me

Born in 2010, I currently study at Bukit Batok Secondary School. I lead as Student Conductor and EXCO (Head of Logistics) in the school choir.

I am deeply passionate about Artificial Intelligence, focusing on: Theoretical & Empirical Analysis of Deep Representations and Modular Networks in Self-Supervised & Multi-Modal Learning, with emphasis on Generalization, Expressivity, and Efficient Lifelong Learning. I also explore theoretical mathematics regarding Combinatorics and Topology applied in AI.

Key Keywords: expressivity, generalization, modular architectures, SSL, multi-modal, pruning, manifold geometry, OOD, scaling laws
Goal: Translate theoretical insights into applied experiments and benchmarks, producing interpretable, predictive metrics for AI system efficiency and generalization.

Beyond STEM, I play the violin (currently ABRSM Grade 8, taking Diploma soon), enjoy public speaking and emceeing events, and have minor experience in 3D CAD modelling using SolidWorks. I also practice rock climbing (bouldering) and calisthenics, and listen to a wide variety of music genres.

On the lighter side, I love exploring music, especially works by Bach, Vivaldi, Handel, Tchaikovsky, Rachmaninoff and more. Music helps me think while generating solutions for research, work and code.
